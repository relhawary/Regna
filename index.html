Overview

Dewpoint is a private firm with 35 years of specialization in HVAC (Heating Ventilation Air-Condition) systems and equipment. Along with quality service, it possess a large portfolio of established clients and employ high caliber, well trained technicians fully equipped with advanced tools and methodologies.

  • Providing successfully executed central air-conditioning systems with capacities exceeding 1000 ton refrigeration

  • Operation and maintenance of electro mechanical systems

  • Diagnostic and repair of air-conditioning and mechanical systems

  • Supply and installation of all kinds and models of air conditioning equipment

  • Supply of spare parts and consumables

Vision

A standard of talent, service, and character that leaves a positive impact on the industry for generations to come.

Mission

Founded in 1980, Dewpoint sets regional HVAC standards through the contracting and maintenance services offered to various clientele across multiple industries.
